The Market

Right now there are over 530 million digital music consumers in China alone.


Accessing this new consumer market is no more exclusive business of large record labels. With the help of TopDMC’s Global Copyright Management Platform Chinese market is now accessible and available also for independent artists and record labels at minimum cost.


More than ever western music is becoming a significant trend in Chinese market. We, at TopDMC, would like to benefit this trend and assist you in finding demand for new music.

Musician is our priority

Our promise is to put musicians’ interest in the first place. We do have a common interest since this benefits our content production. We offer an easy and controlled way for artists to earn royalties.

Copyright holder retains full ownership and control to their immaterial rights.

With no strings copyright holders are free to make their preferred artistic choices.

Market intelligence

At TopDMC we provide artists also with an easy access to consumer information, preferences and updated market data when convenient.

Our platform provides full transparency with real-time updates on daily statistics, including the amount of streams, downloads, and earned royalties among other information.
